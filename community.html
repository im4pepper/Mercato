Doing more in life

We like to do the things that make us happy. We believe in doing things to help others be happy too. Mercato Graphic Design

is actively engaged with community organizations who serve the people around them. We provide design and support to organizations

such as Sons of Baseball Foundation, and Millie's Princess Foundation. Take some time to learn about these organizations. Even if

you don't support their causes, we encourage you to find one you do support ... and then do something to be a part of it.
